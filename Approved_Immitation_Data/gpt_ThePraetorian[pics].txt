Instead, focus on making small, sustainable changes to your diet and exercise routine. Maybe start by swapping out sugary drinks for water, or taking a 10-minute walk after dinner. Every little bit counts. But don't just take my word for it. Do your own research and educate yourself on what works best for your body and your goals.
